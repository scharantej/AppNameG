Welcome to Arsenal Football Club

Arsenal Football Club is a professional football club based in Holloway, London, England. The club was founded in 1886 and has been playing at Emirates Stadium since 2006. Arsenal is one of the most successful clubs in English football, having won 13 First Division/Premier League titles, 14 FA Cups, and 2 League Cups.
